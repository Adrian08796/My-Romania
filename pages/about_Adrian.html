Welcome to Adrian R. Vasiu's blog page!

What is this website about

"My country and myself" is a personal blog relating details and experiences about the lovely country of Romania. More specifically wonderful places to visit, national inherritage, good food and historical places.

My Mission

To bring more awareness and curiosity to people looking to visit this Eastern European jewel.

Who am i?

My name is Adrian. I was born and raised in Sighisoara, home of one of the last remaining inhabited citadels of Europe, in the heart of Transylvania, home of the Infamous bloodthirsty Vlad Dracul also known as Dracula.

My Motto
